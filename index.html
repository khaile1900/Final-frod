Fun Corn Fact:

Corn in Ancient Civilizations

One interesting discovery about corn comes from ancient Mesoamerican societies. Archaeologists have uncovered corn kernels that are over 5,000 years old in some of the oldest known settlements in Mexico. These ancient kernels were often found with evidence of early grinding tools, suggesting that corn was one of the first grains to be ground into flour for use in everyday cooking. It's fascinating how the techniques used to prepare corn thousands of years ago are still relevant today in recipes like corn tortillas, cornbread, and corn pudding.

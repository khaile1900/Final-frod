CORN PUDDING

  • 2 cups fresh or frozen corn kernels
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch (optional for thickening)

Instructions

1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9x9-inch baking dish.
2. In a blender or food processor, blend the corn, milk, and heavy cream until smooth.
3. In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs, melted butter, sugar, salt, and pepper.
4. Add the pureed corn mixture to the egg mixture and stir to combine.
5. If you want a thicker consistency, mix the cornstarch with a little cold water and stir it into the pudding mixture
6. Pour the mixture into the prepared dish and bake for 40-45 minutes, until set and golden on top.
7. Let it cool slightly before serving.

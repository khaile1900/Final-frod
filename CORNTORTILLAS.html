CORN TORTILLAS

  • 2 cups masa harina (corn flour)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 cups warm water

Instructions

1. In a large bowl, mix the masa harina and salt. Gradually add warm water, stirring until a dough forms. If the dough is too dry, add a bit more water; if itâ€™s too wet, add more masa harina.
2. Divide the dough into 10-12 small balls.
3. Roll each ball between two pieces of plastic wrap or parchment paper to form a thin circle.
4. Heat a cast-iron skillet or griddle over medium-high heat. Cook each tortilla for about 1 minute on each side, until lightly browned and cooked through.
5. Serve warm with your favorite fillings.
